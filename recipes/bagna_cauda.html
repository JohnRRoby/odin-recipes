Bagna Cauda

Bagna Cauda is a hot Italian dish made with garlic and anchovies

This recipe (from Allrecipes.com) is mainly used in small servings and is great as an appetizer spread on bread or as a dip with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup minced garlic
  • 4 (2 ounce) cans anchovy fillets packed in olive oil, drained
  • 3 (4 ounce) cans sardines packed in olive oil, drained
  • 1 cup butter

Steps

  1. Place vegetable oil in a skillet and heat over medium heat.
  2. Stir in garlic; cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add butter, anchovies, and sardines; cook and stir until well blended, 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Serve warm.
