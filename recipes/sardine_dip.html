Sardine Dip

Sardine Dip is guaranteed to surprise and delight your guests

This recipe (from Allrecipes.com) is a treat served with crackers, crostini, or bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans sardines in oil
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup real mayonnaise
  • 3 green onions, chopped, green and white parts
  • 1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon seafood seasoning, (such as Old BayÂ® Original)

Steps

  1. Combine sardines, cream cheese, mayonnaise, green onions, mustard, fish sauce, and Old Bay seasoning in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Mix well by hand or with an electric mixer.
